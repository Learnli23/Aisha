Student Attrition System

Vision: To become a center of excellence in education and training to produce employable and innovative graduates.

Mission: To provide quality university education, training, and research to meet the needs of the community and the nation.

Objectives: To increase student retention and reduce dropout rates through data-driven monitoring.

Users: Lecturers, Admins, and Students with tailored access levels.

Why Student Attrition Matters

Student attrition impacts university rankings, funding, and overall quality of education. Our system tracks and analyzes student retention to help improve academic success and institutional reputation.
